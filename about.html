About Kaylan Luxe Salon

A passion for beauty, a commitment to excellence, and a love for our community.

How it began

Our Story

Kaylan Luxe Salon was born from a simple but powerful belief — that every person deserves to feel beautiful, confident, and cared for. Founded in Gaborone, Botswana, the salon was established with a vision to bring a premium beauty experience to the local community.

What started as a small, intimate studio has grown into one of Gaborone's most trusted beauty destinations. Over the years, we have built lasting relationships with our clients, becoming not just a salon, but a place of comfort, creativity, and transformation.

We take great pride in staying current with the latest beauty trends and techniques, continuously training our team to deliver world-class results using professional-grade products.

What drives us

Mission & Vision

◆

Our Mission

To provide every client with an exceptional, personalised beauty experience in a welcoming and professional environment. We are committed to using quality products, skilled techniques, and genuine care to help every person look and feel their very best.

◆

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and celebrated beauty salon in Botswana — known for our excellence, our warmth, and our ability to make every client feel like the best version of themselves. We aspire to grow as a brand that champions confidence and self-care across our community.
